Data collected

We will record your answers to a number of questions. We will also record your Prolific ID to be able to pay you later. We will not collect any sensitive data.

Use of data

The data will be analysed for an academic study.

Data storage

The data will be stored on our own servers, to which only the researchers involved in this study have access. We will keep your data to use for future research.

Anonymity

No personally identifiable information will be recorded apart from the Prolific ID. The Prolific ID will only be seen by the researchers involved in this study. We may share your research data with other investigators without asking for your consent again, but it will not contain information that could directly identify you.

Voluntary participation

It is totally up to you to decide to be in this research study. Participating in this study is voluntary. Even if you decide to be part of the study now, you may change your mind and stop at any time. You do not have to answer any questions you do not want to answer. If you decide to withdraw before this study is completed, please exit and return your submission on Prolific by clicking the 'Stop without completing' button. Your data will be removed from the dataset before analysis begins.
